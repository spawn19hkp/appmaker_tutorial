English

In today’s video, I’ll show you exactly how to enable the Card Payment option for your applications using App Maker. Why limit your app subscriptions to just mobile carrier billing? By integrating the Genie payment gateway, you can allow anyone to subscribe to your services using their Credit or Debit cards.

In this video, we cover:

  • ✅ How to fill out the App Maker card payment request form
  • ✅ Configuring ‘Genie’ in your Operator Configurations
  • ✅ Setting up monthly subscription amounts
  • ✅ How your users can easily pay using their Google accounts and cards
